A Tradition of Excellence, A Vision for Tomorrow

Founded in 1968, Silverbroke High School was established with the vision of creating a modern learning environment where academic excellence meets personal growth. What started as a small community school with just 120 students and 12 teachers has grown into one of the regionâ€™s most respected high schools, now welcoming more than 1,200 students each year. Throughout the 1970s and 1980s, Silverbroke became known for its strong focus on science, arts, and athletics, producing award-winning debate teams, championship-level sports programs, and innovative science fair projects.

In 1995, the school underwent a major campus expansion, adding a state-of-the-art library, science laboratories, and a performing arts center, further solidifying its reputation as a hub of creativity and knowledge. With the rise of technology in the 2000s, Silverbroke High School embraced digital learning, introducing smart classrooms, coding programs, and international exchange partnerships with schools across Europe and Asia.

Today, Silverbroke High School continues to uphold its founding values: excellence, innovation, and community. Our alumni include leaders, artists, entrepreneurs, and scholars who continue to shape the world around them. At Silverbroke, we believe that education is not only about preparing students for exams but also about preparing them for life.
